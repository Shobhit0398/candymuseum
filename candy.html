MUSEUM OF CANDY

In early 2019, New York City will see yet another food-themed museum filled with opportunities for munching, shopping and selfie-taking. Named the Candy Museum, this 30,000-square-foot candy wonderland—located in the iconic Limelight Club space on the corner of 20th Street and Sixth Avenue—is first of three museums founded by Sugar Factory.
